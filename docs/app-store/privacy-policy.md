# ShromSpeak Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 3, 2026

ShromSpeak is an offline text-to-speech utility for iPhone. The current version uses the local speech voices available on the user's device.

## Data Collection

ShromSpeak does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal data.

The text entered into the app is processed locally on the device for speech playback. The current App Store version does not send this text to our servers or to third-party cloud services.

## Network Use

The current App Store version does not require an internet connection for its core text-to-speech function.

## Analytics and Tracking

ShromSpeak does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

## Future Cloud Voices

If ShromSpeak adds optional cloud voices in a future version, this privacy policy and the App Store privacy details will be updated before release. Cloud voices may require sending text to a voice provider only after the user chooses that feature.
